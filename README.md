# Founding Engineer Technical Case

**Overall Goal**

Build an agent-based system that meaningfully accelerates TechCorp's month-end close process by learning from their historical data, institutional knowledge, and processes to solve a core pain point while maintaining compliance and human oversight.

**Inputs/Data**

[User Interviews](user-interviews/) - 3 stakeholder perspectives (Controller, Senior Accountant, CFO)

[Company Context](context/) - Business overview, accounting policies, reconciliation process

[Transaction Data](data/gl_transactions/) - November & December 2025 GL detail

[Prior Work](data/prior_reconciliations/) - Completed reconciliations from October & November (reference examples, poor → good quality)

[Supporting Documents](data/supporting_documents/) - Invoices, contracts, emails, Slack conversations, calculation memos

[Verifiability Rules](data/rules_and_constraints.md) - 35+ quality rules reconciliations must satisfy

**Your call**

From the interviews and data, identify a high-value pain point to solve. Multiple valid directions exist.

**Build**

Stack is open-ended. Use whatever works best.

**What good looks like**

Given the limited time available for this case study, you will need to make deliberate trade-offs about where to focus your efforts. Your prioritization decisions and the reasoning behind them are as important as the solution you deliver and how you execute it.

## Timeboxing

Assume you need to deliver a PoC next day. Depth and end-to-end clarity on one problem beat superficial coverage of many. Ship working code with a clear demo, not perfect code with no demo.

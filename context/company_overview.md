# TechCorp Inc. - Company Overview

## Business Description

**Company**: TechCorp Inc. (fictional company for this case study)
**Industry**: Enterprise Software / SaaS
**Founded**: 2015
**Headquarters**: San Francisco, CA
**Employees**: 850 (as of Dec 2025)

TechCorp provides cloud-based enterprise software solutions for mid-market and enterprise customers. The company offers:
- **Core Platform**: Workflow automation and business process management software (SaaS)
- **Professional Services**: Implementation consulting, custom development, training
- **Support & Maintenance**: Technical support subscriptions

## Financial Profile

**Revenue (FY2025 estimated)**: ~$295M
- Subscription Revenue: 72%
- Professional Services: 22%
- Product Sales (licenses): 6%

**Business Model**: Recurring SaaS subscriptions (annual and multi-year contracts) with professional services for implementation.

**Key Markets**: North America (65%), EMEA (25%), APAC (10%)

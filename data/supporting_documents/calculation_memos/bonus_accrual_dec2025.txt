PURPOSE:
Calculate accrued liability for FY2025 annual bonuses earned but unpaid as of December 31, 2025.

BACKGROUND:
TechCorp has a performance-based bonus program for eligible employees. Bonuses are based on:
- Company performance (revenue and EBITDA targets)
- Individual performance ratings
- Tenure and role level

Bonuses are paid in Q1 2026 (February payroll) but must be accrued in FY2025 per ASC 710 (compensation expense recognized when earned).

STEP 2: COMPANY PERFORMANCE MODIFIER

FY2025 Targets vs. Actuals:
- Revenue Target: $290M | Actual: $294.5M (101.6% of target)
- EBITDA Target: $45M | Actual: $47.2M (104.9% of target)

Performance modifier per comp committee:
- 100-105% of target → 1.0x modifier
- 105-110% of target → 1.1x modifier
- 110%+ of target → 1.2x modifier

Combined performance: (101.6% + 104.9%) / 2 = 103.3%
→ Company modifier: 1.0x
